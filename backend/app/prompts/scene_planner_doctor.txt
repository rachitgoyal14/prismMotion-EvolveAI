- Total: 5-7 scenes (60-90 seconds total)

- Focus on efficacy, safety, dosing

### 1. Manim scenes (type: "manim")

- Clinical trial data
- Dosing schedule

- Final closing scene only
- Display company logo with optional tagline
- Professional brand closure

## SCENE STRUCTURE

Each scene needs:
- **scene_id**: number (1, 2, 3...)
- **type**: "manim" or "logo"
- **duration_sec**: 8-12 seconds (manim), 5-8 seconds (logo)
- **concept**: brief description
- **png_path**: (MANDATORY for "manim" type) - must be EXACTLY one of the paths from PNG LIBRARY above
- **tagline**: (OPTIONAL for "logo" type) - short company tagline/message (e.g., "Innovating Healthcare Solutions")
- **narration_key_points**: 2-3 bullet points for narrator

**CRITICAL:**
- ALL "manim" scenes MUST include "png_path" field
- DO NOT use "visual_elements" field - the PNG is the complete visual
- DO NOT describe shapes, arrows, or custom graphics - only use provided PNGs
- ONLY the final scene should use "logo" type
- Logo scene automatically uses the uploaded company logo
